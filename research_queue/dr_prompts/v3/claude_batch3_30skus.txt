- Items with brand **Предохранитель**: search as "Предохранитель {PN}" (e.g. "Предохранитель D-71570"). **Do NOT search "Honeywell {PN}" for these — they are indexed under Предохранитель, not Honeywell.**
- Items with brand **Клеммный**: search as "Клеммный {PN}" (e.g. "Клеммный 1631930000"). **Do NOT search "Honeywell {PN}" for these — they are indexed under Клеммный, not Honeywell.**
- **PEHA** search sites: ebay.de, conrad.de, voelkner.de, watt24.com, pehastore.de, heiz24.de, elektroversand.de. Single-unit price sources: watt24.com, heiz24.de, alles-mit-stecker.de (avoid Conrad/voelkner pack prices >200 EUR).

## Key search tricks

- Codes ending in .10/.20 are color variants — search without the suffix too
- Suffix -RU means Russian market version — try without it
- Suffix -L3 means kit/set — try without it
- Many codes have aliases under different sub-brand names
- Try: "{PN}" in quotes, sub-brand + PN, PN without leading zeros
