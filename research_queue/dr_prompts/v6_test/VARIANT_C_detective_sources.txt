## Search protocol (follow this EXACTLY for each PN)

**Step 1 — Identify.** Google `"{PN}"` in quotes. Open top 3-5 results. Determine: what is this product? Which series? Which manufacturer division?

**Step 2 — Official docs.** Google `"{PN}" datasheet filetype:pdf`. Check manufacturer site. Find the official datasheet PDF URL.

**Step 3 — Specifications.** From datasheet or product page, extract: dimensions, weight, voltage, power, protocols, IP rating, temperature range, certifications.

**Step 4 — Pricing.** Search in this order:
- Russian stores: vseinstrumenti.ru, lemanapro.ru, etm.ru, electropara.ru, tinko.ru
- European distributors: distrelec.com, mouser.com, rs-online.com
- eBay: ebay.de, ebay.com (surplus/used pricing is acceptable)
- Google Shopping: `"{PN}" buy`
Record the FIRST real price you find with exact URL.

**Step 5 — Photos.** Find a clear product photo. Prefer: manufacturer site > distributor > eBay listing.

**Step 6 — EAN/GTIN.** Search `"{PN}" EAN` or `"{PN}" GTIN`. Check barcodelookup.com.

**Step 7 — Log sources.** Record EVERY URL you actually visited for this PN, with what you found there.
