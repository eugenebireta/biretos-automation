## Search protocol

For each PN:
1. Google `"{PN}"` in quotes
2. Google `"{PN}" datasheet filetype:pdf`
3. Google `"{PN}" price` or `"{PN}" buy`
4. If PN has suffix (.10, -RU, -L3): also try base PN without suffix
5. Check: ebay.de, ebay.com (photos + surplus pricing)
6. Check Russian sources: vseinstrumenti.ru, lemanapro.ru, etm.ru, electropara.ru, tinko.ru
7. Check manufacturer sites: saia-burgess.com, trendcontrols.com, esser-systems.com
